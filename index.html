The Atelier

"A garment should hold still when the room moves — that's the whole philosophy, really."

Peaceful Hautecouture began in 2016 as a single sewing table in the back of a fabric shop. Today it's a twelve-seat atelier where every piece is drafted, cut, and finished by hand — no more than four fittings a week, by design. Founder Elena Vela trained in pattern-cutting before turning to womenswear, and still drafts every house pattern herself.
