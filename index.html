About Me

I am a data professional passionate about transforming complex data into meaningful insights that drive informed decision-making. With a background in Computer science, I specialize in building interactive dashboards and data-driven stories that make information accessible, actionable, and impactful. I enjoy solving business problems with data and transforming raw datasets into clear, actionable insights that support better decisions.

My expertise includes Power BI, SQL, Python, Tableau, Excel, Power Query, DAX, and data modeling, enabling me to build interactive dashboards and analytical solutions that reveal trends, identify opportunities, and communicate insights effectively. Whether supporting business growth, improving operational efficiency, or solving complex challenges, my goal is to use analytics to deliver practical, impactful solutions.
